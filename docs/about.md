## About O.S. Electric

Learn more about **O.S. Electric**, a family-owned electrical contractor serving **Buffalo, New York**, and the surrounding areas. With over **20 years of experience**, we provide licensed, code-compliant electrical services focused on safety, reliability, and long-term performance. Our reputation throughout the Buffalo area is built on consistent workmanship, professional standards, and dependable service our customers can trust.

---

### Built to Last

At O.S. Electric, our work is built to last. As a family-owned Buffalo electrical company, we take pride in delivering high-quality electrical solutions that stand the test of time. Every project is completed with careful attention to detail, strict adherence to electrical codes, and a commitment to long-term system performance.

---

### Project Expertise

Our team brings extensive experience across a wide range of projects, from specialized installations to large-scale commercial environments. We have completed electrical work for healthcare facilities as well as major public and commercial institutions, including **Fashion Outlets of Niagara Falls USA** and **Walden Galleria Mall**. This breadth of experience allows us to confidently manage complex electrical systems while maintaining the highest safety standards.

---

### Dependable Service

Beyond experience, O.S. Electric is known for dependable service our customers can count on throughout **Buffalo, Lancaster, and surrounding communities**. Our licensed **Master and Journeyman electricians** complete every project in accordance with current electrical codes and professional standards, with a focus on consistency, reliability, and long-term performance.
