Who We Are

O.S. Construction is a family-owned and operated construction company based in Buffalo, New York. Founded in 1992, we have built our reputation on quality craftsmanship, dependable service, and attention to detail across every phase of construction.

With decades of hands-on experience, our team delivers professional construction services for residential and commercial projects throughout the Buffalo area. From structural work to detailed finishes, we take pride in building solutions that are durable, functional, and built to last.

Our Experience & Approach

At O.S. Construction, every project is approached with careful planning, skilled execution, and a commitment to doing the job right. We believe in clear communication, realistic timelines, and workmanship that meets or exceeds industry standards.

Whether we are handling new construction, renovations, or structural upgrades, our focus remains the same: delivering reliable results while maintaining a clean, safe, and organized job site.

Why Choose O.S. Construction

What sets O.S. Construction apart is our combination of experience, professionalism, and personal service. As a smaller, family-run company, we are able to provide hands-on involvement and responsive communication throughout every project.

Our clients trust us because we stand behind our work, respect their property, and deliver construction solutions designed for long-term value and performance.

Serving Buffalo & Surrounding Areas

O.S. Construction proudly serves homeowners, businesses, and property owners throughout Buffalo, NY and the surrounding communities. From small projects to larger construction work, we bring the same level of care, precision, and professionalism to every job.
